Privacy Policy

Carboy — Homebrew Recipe Manager  |  Last updated: June 15, 2025

No Data Collection

Carboy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

All recipes, photos, and preferences you enter are stored exclusively on your device using the browser's local storage (WebView DOM storage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Internet Access Required

Carboy works entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests. The INTERNET permission declared in the manifest is present solely as a precaution for the WebView component and is not actively used.

Backups

The backup feature exports your recipes to a JSON file saved locally on your device. You control where that file goes; we have no access to it.

Third-Party Services

Carboy uses no third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Carboy does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new date.

Contact
